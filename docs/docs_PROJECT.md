# Project Overview

## The Problem

**B2B Cash Flow Crisis:**
- Suppliers typically wait **30-90 days** for invoice payment
- This creates severe cash flow problems for businesses
- **$5 trillion** in B2B invoices globally awaiting payment
- Small/medium businesses often can't afford to wait
- Traditional invoice factoring is expensive (10-15% fees) and slow

**Current Solutions Are Broken:**
- Banks charge high fees and require extensive paperwork
- Factoring companies take large cuts (10-15%)
- No transparency or fraud prevention
- Manual processes take days/weeks
- No programmable, onchain settlement

---

## Our Solution

**AI-Powered Invoice NFT Marketplace on BNB Chain**

A multi-agent AI system that automatically:

1. **Verifies invoices** - Gemini AI extracts and validates invoice data
2. **Detects fraud** - Pattern recognition identifies suspicious invoices
3. **Tokenizes as NFTs** - Converts approved invoices into tradeable digital assets
4. **AI pricing** - Calculates optimal discount based on risk and time factors
5. **Onchain settlement** - Smart contracts handle trustless transactions

### Key Innovation

**Multi-Agent AI Orchestration:**
- **Verification Agent**: Parses PDFs, extracts structured data, calculates hash
- **Fraud Detection Agent**: Scores risk (0-100), filters problematic invoices
- **Tokenization Agent**: AI determines marketplace viability, calculates pricing
- **Reconciliation Agent**: Monitors payments, triggers NFT redemption

**Only approved invoices reach blockchain** - rejected ones are filtered by AI before any onchain transaction occurs.
